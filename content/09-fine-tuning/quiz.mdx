## Key Terms

### Drift

Simple meaning:
Behavior changing away from intended outcomes.

Real-world analogy:
Performance changes after repeated coaching without tests.

Technical meaning:
Distribution shift across data, prompts, and use-cases.

Example:
Model starts violating tone policy after new examples.

Why it matters:
Drift can silently erode trust.

## Mental Model

Adaptation is a controlled experiment with monitoring, not one-time training.
