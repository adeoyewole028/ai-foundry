# Cost Control

## Objective

By the end of this lesson, you can reduce AI spend without harming trust.

## Prerequisites

Before this lesson, complete Lesson 05 in this module.

## Plain-English Explanation

Cost control uses model routing, caching, and quotas to avoid unexpected spend.

## Key Terms

### Quota

Simple meaning:
Budget limit for usage.

Real-world analogy:
Credit card daily limit for unexpected spending.

Technical meaning:
Guardrail that caps call counts and token usage.

Example:
Per-user token cap with escalation above threshold.

Why it matters:
Finances and reliability are part of user trust.

## Mental Model

Allocate cost budgets to quality tiers, not just raw request volume.

## Example

Use a cheap model for triage and escalate complex cases.

## Code Walkthrough

Add budget, model-router, and cache invalidation strategy.

## Common Mistakes

- Cost optimization done after incidents.
- No cache strategy for repeated requests.
- Ignoring model routing side effects.

## Exercise

Design a token budget policy with warning and hard-stop levels.

## Reflection

How do you avoid optimizing only for cost at the expense of quality?
