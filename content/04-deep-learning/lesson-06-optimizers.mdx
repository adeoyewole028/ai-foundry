# Optimizers

## Objective

By the end of this lesson, you will compare optimizer behavior and choose practical defaults.

## Prerequisites

Before this lesson, you should understand gradients and update rules.

## Plain-English Explanation

Optimizers decide how large and in what direction to move model weights.

## Key Terms

### Learning rate

Simple meaning:
How big the step size is each update.

Real-world analogy:
Walking toward a target with big or small steps.

Technical meaning:
Hyperparameter that scales gradient-based updates.

Example:
Adam adapts step sizes based on past gradients.

Why it matters:
Step size and momentum affect speed and stability.
