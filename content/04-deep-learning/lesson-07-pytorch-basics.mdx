## Plain-English Explanation

PyTorch gives you composable building blocks for models, data, loss, and training loops.

## Key Terms

### Tensor

Simple meaning:
A multi-dimensional array for numeric compute.

Real-world analogy:
Like a structured spreadsheet that can grow in dimensions.

Technical meaning:
An n-dimensional array with GPU support and autograd integration.

Example:
Representing image batches as `(batch, channels, height, width)`.

Why it matters:
Most deep-learning tooling assumes tensor-first workflows.

## Common Mistakes

- Forgetting `model.train()` and `model.eval()` modes.
- Forgetting to reset gradients.
- Using wrong tensor shapes for batches.
