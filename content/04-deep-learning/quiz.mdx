## Key Terms

### Hyperparameters

Simple meaning:
Settings you choose before training.

Real-world analogy:
Recipe settings for heat, time, and servings.

Technical meaning:
Learning rate, architecture size, epochs, optimizer choices, and regularization.

Example:
Adjusting batch size and learning rate together.

Why it matters:
Small tuning choices drastically change learning behavior.
