# Module 00 Overview: AI Foundations

This module introduces AI from first principles before you move into engineering.

## What you’ll learn

- The difference between AI, machine learning, deep learning, and LLMs.
- How models learn from data and why data quality matters.
- Core vocabulary used in most AI products and projects.
- Why RAG, agents, and guardrails matter for practical AI systems.

## Learning loop

You will move through:

1. Concept
2. Simple explanation
3. Example
4. Code walkthrough
5. Exercise
6. Project
7. Reflection
