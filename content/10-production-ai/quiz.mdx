## Key Terms

### SLO Breach

Simple meaning:
Missing a promised reliability target.

Real-world analogy:
Missed email delivery target in a customer service platform.

Technical meaning:
Measured indicator crossing the accepted service threshold.

Example:
Latency p95 above target for 2 consecutive windows.

Why it matters:
Reliable products need early response before customer trust erodes.
