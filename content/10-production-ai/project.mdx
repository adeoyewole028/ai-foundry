## Key Terms

### SLO

Simple meaning:
Service level objective.

Real-world analogy:
Store promise: order fulfillment under 30 minutes.

Technical meaning:
Quantified target for latency and reliability.

Example:
99.5% requests under 2 seconds with &lt;1% fallback.

Why it matters:
SLOs align engineering and business expectations.
