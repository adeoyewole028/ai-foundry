## Key Terms

### Predictive workflow

Simple meaning:
How data becomes a forecast with checks and limits.

Real-world analogy:
You do not just build, you also review and explain your forecast.

Technical meaning:
Feature design, training, validation, metric tracking, and deployment-aware decisions.

Example:
Scoring candidates for job matches with clear validation.

Why it matters:
Confidence comes from repeatability and proper evaluation, not memorized formulas.
