# Pandas

## Objective

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to inspect, filter, and transform tabular data.

## Prerequisites

Before this lesson, you should understand CSV basics and simple Python functions.

## Plain-English Explanation

Pandas is your table operator. It lets you answer business questions from columns and rows.

## Key Terms

### DataFrame

Simple meaning:
Think of it as a spreadsheet in Python.

Real-world analogy:
Rows are records; columns are your questions.

Technical meaning:
`DataFrame` holds labeled, mutable tabular data with axis labels.

Example:
Filtering late-delivery rows to compute reliability rates.

Why it matters:
Consistent tabular operations are foundational for AI data prep.

## Mental Model
