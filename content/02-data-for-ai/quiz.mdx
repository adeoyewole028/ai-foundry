## Key Terms

### Evaluation integrity

Simple meaning:
Your test results should match real behavior outside your setup.

Real-world analogy:
Mock interviews only validate rehearsal, not the final interview.

Technical meaning:
Split strategy and leakage controls preserve honest metric estimates.

Example:
Using full-data normalization before splitting can leak stats.

Why it matters:
Integrity protects user trust and product decisions.
