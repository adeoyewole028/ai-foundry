## Key Terms

### Boundary

Simple meaning:
The edges of what an agent is allowed to do.

Real-world analogy:
Company policy around who can approve spending.

Technical meaning:
Runtime controls around tools, memory, and approval thresholds.

Example:
Blocking external posting unless approved by user.

Why it matters:
Without boundaries, power grows faster than control.

## Mental Model

Safe autonomy = clear intention, bounded tools, explicit stop points, observable outcomes.
