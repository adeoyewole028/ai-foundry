## Example

Scenario: "A support bot needs to find the most relevant help article."

Answer: Use vectors to represent the question and articles, then dot product or another similarity score to rank matches.

## Code Walkthrough

```python
question = [0.7, 0.2, 0.5]
article = [0.6, 0.1, 0.6]

score = sum(a * b for a, b in zip(question, article))
print(score)
```

This tiny score stands in for a larger retrieval step.

## Common Mistakes

- Naming a concept without explaining why it fits.
- Treating confidence as certainty.
- Forgetting that training needs feedback from mistakes.
